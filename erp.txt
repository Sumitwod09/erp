LUMIERE ERP - System Overview & Features
==========================================

CONCEPT
-------
Lumiere ERP is a state-of-the-art, modular Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system designed for desktop environments. Unlike traditional rigid ERPs, Lumiere is "Industry-Adaptive," allowing businesses to toggle specific modules on or off based on their unique operational needs.

This flexibility ensures that a retail store only sees retail tools, while a manufacturing unit has access to production planning and BOMsâ€”all within the same core framework.

CORE ARCHITECTURE
-----------------
- Modular Design: Features are partitioned into self-contained modules that can be activated on-demand.
- Industry Presets: Pre-configured templates for Retail, Manufacturing, Services, and more.
- RBAC (Role-Based Access Control): Granular permission system ensuring secure data access.
- Cloud-First Backend: Powered by Nhost (GraphQL / PostgreSQL) for real-time synchronization and scalability.
- Cross-Platform UI: Built with Flutter for a premium, high-performance desktop experience.

DETAILED FEATURES
-----------------

1. INVENTORY MANAGEMENT
   - Real-time stock tracking across multiple warehouses.
   - Low-stock alerts and automated reordering points.
   - Batch and serial number tracking.

2. SALES & POINT-OF-SALE (POS)
   - Streamlined quotation and order processing.
   - Interactive POS interface for quick retail transactions.
   - Customer management and sales history tracking.

3. INVOICING & BILLING
   - Professional invoice generation with customizable templates.
   - Automated due date reminders.
   - Integration with sales for one-click invoice creation.

4. ACCOUNTING & LEDGER
   - Double-entry bookkeeping system.
   - Real-time financial reports (Profit/Loss, Balance Sheet).
   - Expense tracking and bank reconciliation.

5. CRM (Customer Relationship Management)
   - Lead and opportunity management.
   - Interaction logs and customer support ticket tracking.
   - Sales pipeline visualization.

6. HRM & PAYROLL
   - Employee records and document management.
   - Automated payroll calculation with tax compliance.
   - Attendance and leave tracking.

7. MANUFACTURING (BOM)
   - Multi-level Bill of Materials (BOM) management.
   - Work order processing and production scheduling.
   - Raw material consumption tracking.

TECHNICAL STACK
---------------
- Frontend: Flutter (Dart)
- State Management: Riverpod
- Backend: Nhost (Hasura GraphQL Engine, Postgres, Auth)
- UI Library: Material Design 3 with custom premium aesthetics.
